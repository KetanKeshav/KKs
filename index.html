Publications

  • Title: Ensemble learning as a prerogative method of predicting mortality of patients with cardiovascular diseases
  • Name of Presenting Organization: IEEE International Conference on Electronics, Computing and Communication Technologies (CONECCT)
  • Issued Date: 07 December 2021
  • Brief Description: I conducted research in collaboration with my professor and peer from my undergrad college. The dataset we pursued originally had patient information with diseases extraneous to CVD like respiratory problems, cardiovascular diseases, neurological diseases, with 186 attributes. After cleaning the data, the dataset contained 46 attributes and 38816 patient data records. We used Machine Learning Algorithms - Random Forest, Neural Networks, and Logistic Regression to predict the mortality of the patients. After calibrating the accuracy of each model, we used Ensemble Learning to evaluate the performance of the three Machine Learning Models.
